About Quiver's Ice

Our Journey of Crafting Timeless Elegance

Founded by visionary jeweler Quiver Dollar, Quiver's Ice began as a small atelier with a big dream: to create stunning jewelry that transcends time and trends. What started as a passion for designing unique, hand-crafted pieces has now blossomed into a brand synonymous with luxury, quality, and sophistication.

Our journey is rooted in a deep love for artistry and an unwavering commitment to excellence. Quiver Dollar, our CEO and master jeweler, has always believed that jewelry is more than just adornment. It's an expression of personal style, a marker of life's significant moments, and an embodiment of memories and emotions. This philosophy is the cornerstone of everything we do at Quiver's Ice.

Our Craftsmanship

At Quiver's Ice, we pride ourselves on our meticulous attention to detail and our dedication to the craft of jewelry making. Every piece is created with the utmost care, using only the finest materials sourced from around the world. Our artisans blend traditional techniques with modern innovations to produce jewelry that is both classic and contemporary, elegant yet bold.

We understand that jewelry is an investment, both emotionally and financially. That's why we go above and beyond to ensure that each piece we create meets the highest standards of quality. From the initial design sketches to the final polishing, every step in our process is carried out with precision and passion.

Our Collection

Quiver's Ice offers a diverse collection of jewelry that caters to a wide range of tastes and styles. Whether you're looking for a statement piece that turns heads or a subtle accessory that adds a touch of elegance to your everyday look, we have something for everyone. Our collection includes:

  • Engagement Rings: Crafted to celebrate love and commitment, our engagement rings feature a variety of cuts, settings, and stones, ensuring that every couple can find the perfect symbol of their union.
  • Necklaces and Pendants: From simple, elegant designs to intricate, bold statements, our necklaces and pendants are designed to complement any outfit and occasion.
  • Bracelets: Our bracelets range from delicate chains to robust cuffs, each designed to add a touch of sophistication to your wrist.
  • Earrings: Whether you prefer studs, hoops, or drop earrings, our collection offers something for every taste.
  • Custom Jewelry: For those who desire something truly unique, we offer custom design services. Work with our designers to create a piece that reflects your personal style and story.

Our Commitment to Sustainability

At Quiver's Ice, we believe in responsible luxury. We are committed to ethical sourcing and sustainability in every aspect of our business. We carefully select our materials, ensuring that our gemstones and metals are ethically sourced and environmentally friendly.
