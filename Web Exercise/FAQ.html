Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer custom jewelry design?

Yes, we specialize in creating custom jewelry pieces tailored to your unique vision. Our designers work closely with you to bring your ideas to life.

What materials do you use in your jewelry?

We use only the finest materials, including ethically sourced diamonds, gemstones, and precious metals like gold, platinum, and silver.

How can I care for my jewelry?

Proper care will help maintain the beauty and longevity of your jewelry. We recommend regular cleaning and professional inspections to ensure your pieces remain in excellent condition.

Do you offer financing options?

Yes, we offer flexible financing plans to make your jewelry purchase more manageable. Contact us for more details on available options.

How long does it take to create a custom piece?

The timeline for creating a custom piece varies depending on the complexity of the design. Generally, it takes between 4 to 8 weeks from the initial consultation to the final product.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy on most items, provided they are in their original condition. Custom pieces and engraved items may have different terms. Please review our full return policy or contact us for more information.
