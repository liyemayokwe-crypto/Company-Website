Privacy Policy

At Quiver's Ice, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled in a safe and responsible manner. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your data.

Information We Collect

We may collect personal information from you when you visit our website, make a purchase, or contact us. This information may include your name, email address, phone number, and payment details.

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used to process your orders, respond to inquiries, and improve your shopping experience. We may also use your data to send you promotional materials and updates if you have opted to receive such communications.

How We Protect Your Information

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. This includes secure servers, encrypted transactions, and restricted access to your data.

Third-Party Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties, except when required by law or necessary to fulfill your order (e.g., with our payment processors).

Your Consent

By using our website, you consent to our privacy policy. If we make changes to this policy, we will update this page to reflect those changes.

Contacting Us
